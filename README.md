### Task 5 — Cybercrime Awareness Case Study & Prevention Guide *(20 marks)*

**Part A – Case Study: UPI "Collect Request" Fraud**
Documented how scammers use UPI collect requests on platforms like OLX to trick victims into sending money by entering their UPI PIN. Key takeaway: a UPI PIN is only needed to *send* money, never to receive it.

**Part B – Prevention Checklist: Stay Safe Online**
An 8+ point checklist covering:
- Financial & UPI safety
- Account & password security
- Safe internet usage
- Protection from online scams
- Reporting cybercrime via **cybercrime.gov.in** and helpline **1930**

 See: `task-5-cybercrime/`
