## Digital Ambassador Report: UPI “Collect Request” Fraud Case Study

## Overview
UPI fraud is a common cybercrime in India. Instead of hacking an account, scammers trick people into sending money themselves by using fake payment requests.

## How the Scam Works
Scammers find victims on apps like OLX or social media.
They pretend to be buyers and gain the victim’s trust.
Instead of sending money, they send a collect request through apps like Google Pay or PhonePe.
They tell the victim to enter their UPI PIN or scan a QR code to receive money.
When the victim enters the PIN, money is taken from their account.
Key Takeaway

## Impact and Takeaways
A UPI PIN is only needed to send money, not to receive it. Students and young adults are often targeted because they may not know this rule.
