## 5 Do's

* [ ] Keep your profile information updated and accurate on platforms connected to college or internships.
* [ ] Think about how a post may look to teachers, classmates, or future employers before sharing it.
* [ ] Join educational groups or communities related to coding, technology, and your college subjects.
* [ ] Use strong passwords and enable two-factor authentication on all your social media accounts.
* [ ] Share achievements such as certificates, projects, hackathons, or workshops in a professional way.

## 5 Don'ts

* [ ] Do not post when you are angry or upset, as emotional posts can create misunderstandings.
* [ ] Do not accept friend requests or messages from unknown people without checking their profile first.
* [ ] Do not spend excessive time on social media during classes, study time, or exams.
* [ ] Do not post screenshots of private chats, online classes, or college portals without permission.
* [ ] Do not use fake information, fake accounts, or misleading content that can harm your reputation.
